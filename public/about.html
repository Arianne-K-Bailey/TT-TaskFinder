About TT TaskFinder

Built by students, for the community. Connecting Trinidad & Tobago through local services.

Our Mission

TT TaskFinder was created to connect people who need services with skilled workers across Trinidad & Tobago. From lawn care to technical support, we make it easier to find help.

Many residents struggle to find reliable services, while skilled individuals struggle to find consistent work. Our platform bridges that gap.

We aim to create opportunities, build trust, and strengthen our local community.
